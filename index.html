CASE 01
AI Content Command Center — Autonomous Content Production at Scale

Significant reduction in marketing expenses and increase in content production and data accuracy through AI content creation automation.

A complete end-to-end content production system that researches trends, creates content, generates visuals and video, submits for human review, publishes autonomously across 6 platforms, and learns from performance data — cycling every 8 hours 365x a year, without a social media team.

/ Deep DiveWhat We Built

The Content Command Center — 7 autonomous workflows running in sequence

Built on n8n as the workflow orchestration layer with Claude API (Anthropic) as the intelligence engine. The system operates as a closed-loop, self-correcting production pipeline.

PHASE 1 — RESEARCH
Trend & Competitor Intelligence
+

Overnight, AI scrapes competitor content across social platforms, forecasts trends, and detects viral signals. All research is merged into a structured bundle saved to a central library.

PHASE 2 — RELATE
Brand Alignment & Content Angles
+

AI downloads the research bundle alongside historical performance data. It scores brand-fit against your target audience and generates platform-specific content angles — saving a structured creative brief to the content library.

PHASE 3 — CREATE
Content & Visual Production
+

AI generates platform-specific copy, produces static visuals and video reels, assembles complete content pieces with metadata, and saves everything to the content library — ready for review.

PHASE 4 — REVIEW
Human Review Digest
+

Each morning, AI compiles all created content pieces into a review dashboard and delivers a digest email to the team. The reviewer can approve, reject, or request edits — one action per piece, no login required.

PHASE 5 — PUBLISH
Approval Webhook & Auto-Publish
+

Triggered by reviewer action. AI validates the approval, routes the piece accordingly — publishing approved content across all 6 channels, archiving rejections, and processing edit requests by revising the piece automatically.

PHASE 6 — FEEDBACK
Performance & Learning Loop
+

End of day, AI pulls analytics on every published piece, analyzes performance patterns, extracts content insights, and saves learnings to the library — feeding the next research cycle with real performance data.

PHASE 7 — LIBRARY
Content Library Watcher
+

A persistent background workflow monitoring the Google Drive content library in real time. When new brand assets or guidelines are added, it extracts context and injects new knowledge into the next research cycle — keeping the system aligned with brand evolution without manual reconfiguration.

CASE 02
AI Warm Lead Harvesting Engine — Autonomous Sales Pipeline Generation

Conversion rate and sales team productivity increase through AI warm lead harvesting.

A fully autonomous lead generation system that monitors public channels across the internet, identifies high-intent prospects matching your ideal customer profile, imports them directly into your CRM, and routes each lead to the sales rep most likely to close — before a human ever needs to look for them.

/ Deep DiveWhat We Built

3-Phase Lead Harvesting Workflow — triggered every hour, around the clock

Built on n8n with AI intelligence at each stage. The system monitors public signals continuously, qualifies and scores leads against your ideal customer profile, and routes them to the right rep — with no manual prospecting required.

PHASE 1 — RESEARCH
Multi-Channel Lead Harvesting
+

AI continuously monitors public channels — Reddit, Craigslist, online marketplaces, and social platforms — scanning for outbound signals matching your target criteria. Leads are scored on intent strength, recency, and fit before any human sees them.

PHASE 2 — CRM IMPORT & REP MATCHING
Intelligent Routing & Assignment
+

AI maps each lead's available data into your CRM — contact details, source, intent signal, fit score, and context notes. It assigns the lead to the sales rep most likely to close, based on closing track record, pipeline load, and historical performance patterns.

PHASE 3 — FEEDBACK & LEARNING
Performance Loop & Optimization
+

AI reports pipeline activity to the director of sales on a defined cadence. As outcomes are logged, the system updates its routing model — improving lead-to-rep matching accuracy over time. The longer the system runs, the better it gets.

CASE 03
Inbox Intelligence System — AI-Powered Gmail Triage & Response Automation

A scaling CEO was spending 2 hours a day on email — reading, sorting, drafting, flagging, and filing. At end of Q1, hiring an assistant felt like a problem on top of a problem. We built an Inbox Intelligence System instead.

A Gmail Trigger node fires every time a new email arrives and pulls the full content — subject, sender, body, thread history. Every email passes to Claude via a Basic LLM Chain node. A Structured Output Parser ensures clean output. Claude classifies each email into five categories: urgent / partner / admin / noise / prospect. High-value emails are labeled in Gmail and pushed to Slack with a one-line AI summary. Claude drafts a reply in the CEO's voice using previous thread context. Every Friday at 4pm, a weekly digest delivers priority conversations, follow-ups, and action items. One human step: review, adjust, send.

/ Deep DiveWhat We Built

Inbox Intelligence Workflow — triggered on every inbound email, 24/7

Built on n8n with Gmail API and Claude (Anthropic) as the intelligence layer. The system classifies, summarizes, drafts, and digests — reducing a 2-hour daily email load to a 10-minute review.

PHASE 1 — INGEST
Gmail Trigger & Content Extraction
+

A Gmail Trigger node fires on every new email, pulling subject, sender, body, and full thread history. The raw content is passed to Claude for classification.

PHASE 2 — CLASSIFY
AI Triage & Categorization
+

Claude classifies each email into five categories — urgent, partner, admin, noise, prospect — using a Structured Output Parser for clean, consistent output. High-value emails are labeled in Gmail and pushed to Slack with a one-line AI summary.

PHASE 3 — DRAFT
AI Response Generation
+

Claude drafts a reply in the CEO's voice using previous thread context and tone matching. The draft is staged in Gmail — one human step: review, adjust, send.

PHASE 4 — DIGEST
Weekly Priority Report
+

Every Friday at 4pm, a scheduled workflow compiles the week's priority conversations, pending follow-ups, and action items into a structured digest — delivered to Slack or email.
